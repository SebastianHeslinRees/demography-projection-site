London's population is projected to grow from 8.87 million to between 9.26 million (5-year trend) and 10.17 million (15-year trend) in 2046, with the 10-year trend projection giving 9.83 million. This implies annualised growth of 16 thousand to 54 thousand per year over the 24-year period.
