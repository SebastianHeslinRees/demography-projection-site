## 6 Data sources

### 6.1 Estimates back series

These projections use ONS 2022 Mid-Year Estimates as the starting population. ONS also published a rebased 2012 to 2021 series aligned to the 2021 Census.

The GLA's preferred approach is to use an alternative modelled back series where change is attributed to conventional components (births, deaths and migration), rather than relying on large residual unattributable change values.

Annual estimates of births, deaths and internal migration for years ending mid-2002 to mid-2022 are used unchanged from official series, along with population totals for key census and estimate years. International migration flows are adjusted for consistency with census-year totals.

### 6.2 Other inputs

Other inputs include:
- Projected age-specific mortality rates from ONS interim 2020-based National Population Projections.
- Births by age of mother combined with modelled recent births to estimate local authority fertility rates.
- Provisional year-ending June 2023 UK international migration totals, disaggregated by age, sex and local authority using recent distribution patterns.
