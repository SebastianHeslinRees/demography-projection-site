## 1 Overview of the projections

The GLA Demography team produces annual population projections to support planning and policy across London. The 2022-based projections use the latest ONS Mid-Year Estimate (mid-2022) as their starting point.

This release includes:
- Trend-based projections for all local authority districts in England and Wales to 2050.
- Housing-led projections for electoral wards and local authorities in London to 2041.

No household projections are included in the 2022-based outputs. Household outputs are planned for a later release after the model update that incorporates 2021 Census results.

### 1.1 Variants

Three variants of both trend-based and housing-led projections are published:
- 5-year low fertility
- 10-year central fertility
- 15-year high fertility

These variants differ by migration trend period (5, 10 or 15 years) and fertility assumptions. Mortality assumptions are shared across variants.

Note: throughout this report, variants are referred to by migration trend length (for example, 5-year trend).

### 1.2 Published outputs

Published outputs include:
- Excel workbooks with population by single year of age and sex, plus summary components of change.
- Trend-based outputs for all local authority districts and regions in England and Wales.
- Housing-led outputs for London wards (2022 boundaries) and London local authorities.
- A zip archive of detailed components of change for trend-based projections.
