## 5 Comparisons with 2021-based projections

The 2021-based interim projections were released in January 2023 and were the first to include 2021 Census results.

Key changes in the 2022-based round include:
- Updated methodology for modelled back series estimates.
- Inclusion of revised ONS domestic migration estimates.
- Recalibrated post-pandemic mortality assumptions.
- Fertility held constant at initial levels instead of following projected ONS NPP changes.

The 2021-based range for London's 2046 population was 9.25 million to 10.27 million. The 2022-based range is 9.26 million to 10.17 million.
